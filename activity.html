Doing an activity on what we learn makes us more open to the ideas that we learnt. We have an exciting activity for you. By now you must have understood the concept of pollution, carbon footprint and how to reduce it. You can make any sort of art, craft, painting or click a pic that explains your understanding of what carbon footprint is. This way you will be able to explore more things about this topic and have fun both at the same time. To submit your work upload an image of whatever you have done and press on the submit button.
